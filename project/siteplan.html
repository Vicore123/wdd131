Site Plan Document

Site Name

Name: Digital Aetas

This name was chosen to reflect the chronological evolution of computers throughout history, and Aetas means age in Latim

domain: digitalaetas.com

Site Purpose

Educate users about the history and development of computers, showing a timeline that highlights major milestones, like inventions and innovators from early computing until modern-day technology.

Scenarios

  • Who invented the first programmable computer?
  • How did computers evolve from room-sized machines to smartphones?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color - Raisin Black (#1B1C24): Used for header, footer and typography when needed.
  • Secondary Color - Linen (#F5ECE0): Used for main background color and typography when needed.

Typography

  • Primary Font - Inter: Used for body text and paragraph content.
  • Fallback Font: sans-serif.
